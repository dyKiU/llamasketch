How It Works

3

Features

Live Sketch Mode

Every stroke triggers a new generation after a short idle delay. See your sketch come to life as you draw, no buttons to press.

Creativity Control

Slide between faithful reproduction and wild artistic interpretation. Low creativity keeps close to your sketch; high creativity lets the AI reimagine it.

Variations

Generate up to 128 variations from a single sketch in one click. Browse them in a filmstrip and pick your favorite.

Session History

Every generation is saved locally. Scroll back through your session history, re-use sketches, and compare outputs side by side.
